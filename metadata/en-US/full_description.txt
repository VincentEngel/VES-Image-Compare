Struggling to decide between two nearly identical selfies? Need to track your fitness progress or home renovation? VES Image Compare is the cleanest, fastest way to spot the subtlest differences between two images, right on your device.

We don't track you or clutter your screen with ads. It’s a lightweight tool designed for photographers, designers, and anyone who loves perfection.


Powerful Comparison Modes

* Side by Side: The classic view for a quick visual check.

* Overlay Tap: Stack images and toggle between them with a tap—perfect for spotting "ghosting" or movement.

* Overlay Slide: Use a draggable slider to reveal the "before" and "after" in real-time.

* Transparent Overlay: Blend two images to check alignment or color shifts.

* Overlay Cut: A precision line-cut tool for detailed side-by-side analysis.


Key Features for Better Results

* Synchronized Zoom: Zoom into one image, and the other follows perfectly. Ideal for checking focus and sharpness.

* Privacy First: No internet permission required. Your photos never leave your phone.

* Zero Ads: A clean, distraction-free interface.

* Flexible Loading: Pick from your gallery, use the camera, or "Share to App" directly from your photo browser.

* Pre-Comparison Tools: Rotate and resize images to ensure a fair comparison.

* Before & After: Fitness transformations, makeup looks, or DIY projects.

* Quality Control: Comparing compressed vs. original images.
